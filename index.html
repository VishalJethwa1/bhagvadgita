Bhagvad Gita

gītā su-gītā kartavyā
kim anyaiḥ śāstra-vistaraiḥ
yā svayaṁ padmanābhasya
mukha-padmād viniḥsṛtā

Chunki Bhagvad Gita Bhagwan ke mukh se nikli hai, is liye kisi dusre vedic sahitya ko parhne ki zarurat nahin rehti. Use sirf Bhagvad Gita ko hi dhyan se man laga kar sun'na or parhna chahye. Is yug mein log sansaar ke kaamon mein itne vyast hain ki unke liye sare vedic sahitya ko parh pana mumkin nahin hai. Lekin is ki zarurat bhi nahin hai. Sirf ek pustak, Bhagvad Gita hi kafi hai kyunki yeh sare vedic granthon ka nichor hai or is ka pravachan Bhagwan ne kya hai.
